ABOUT

I am a Doctor of Philisophy(Ph.D.) student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Tezpur University, under the guidance of Prof. Dilip Kumar Saikia and Dr. Nabajyoti Medhi. My research area is in Software-Defined Network and Fog Computing and to add the benefits of both these technologies to solve problems in the Healthcare 4.0 industry. I was awarded the AICTE National Doctoral Fellowship 2019 which enabled me to joined my PhD program in July 2019 at Tezpur University. Prior to PhD, I completed my Master of Technology(M.Tech) from National Institute of Technology, Yupia under Dr. Swarnendu Kumar Chakraborty and completed my Bachelor of Engineering(B.E.) from Annamalai University under the guidance of Dr. T.Suresh.
